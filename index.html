Read the rules first:

  • You start with $500.
  • The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's score without going over 21.
  • Face cards(K/Q/J) are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand.
  • Other cards hold their numerical value.
  • To 'Hit' is to ask for a/another card. To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn.
  • If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand.
  • If you win, you win 1.5 the amount of your bet.
  • Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 15 or higher.
  • This is a simplier version. So, 'Splitting' and 'Doubling down' is not included.
  • If you have no money left to bet then refresh to play again :(
  • Be careful when you are betting ;)
  • To 'Deal' is to bet again.
